Administrator Training Course

Optimize Workflow, Inc provides five different training classes for key Optimize Workflow user types. The training is conducted at Optimize Workflow, Inc corporate headquarters or at the customer’s facility. The following is an outline of the training course for individuals that can access the Optimize Workflow system as an Administrator. The Administrator training course is four hours.
  1. Optimize Workflow Overview
  2. Optimize Workflow Basic Concepts
  3. Optimize Workflow Major Benefits
  4. Administrator Responsibilities
  5. Maintaining Dictionary
  6. Optimize Workflow Demonstration
  7. Creating/Maintaining Inventory
  8. Updating Options
  9. Creating/Updating Users
  10. Administrator Reports
  11. Optimize Workflow Demonstration
  12. Questions and Answers
