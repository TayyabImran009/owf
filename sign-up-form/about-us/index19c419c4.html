ABOUT OP3IT, Inc

OP3IT software was incorporated in the state of California in 2010. The founders of OP3IT, Inc were convinced they could develop a software product that would help organizations conduct successful projects. The OP3IT, Inc technical team spent an extensive five years researching existing products and discussing with executives, managers and project office directors the problems they experienced which caused so many project failures. During this time they then developed an innovative and comprehensive suite of tools that successfully resolves both projects and project portfolios challenges.

OUR MISSION

OP3IT, Inc’s mission is to provide organizations with a project portfolio management tool that increases their future profitability conducting multiple projects annually. Further, it is our goal to assure success of our client’s projects  and increase their ROI by providing a Management tool which ensures all projects satisfy the triple constraint of time, cost and scope.

OUR TEAM

Joel  Fleiss is the founder of OP3IT, Inc and the principal designer of OP3IT.  He is responsible for the product’s overall strategic direction as its Chief Technology Officer. Prior to forming OP3IT, Inc, Mr. Fleiss founded and was the CEO of two software firms, Proprietary Software Systems (PSS) and Quality Information Systems (QIS) for over two decades and worked as a consultant. Mr.Fleiss has a Bachelor in Economics, completed graduate studies in Economics and Computer Science at UCLA and holds the Management Professional (PMP) designation from the Management Institute (PMI). Mr. Fleiss is the author of a project  management book, the “ABCs of Requirements Based  Management” which was the basis of a course he taught at UCLA.

Craig Nagasugi, President OP3IT, Inc, Inc. As the President of OP3IT, Inc, Mr. Nagasugi's primary responsibilitie are marketing and sales activities associated with OP3IT, Inc’ products and services and raising capital funding for HR, operational and sales growth. Prior to OP3IT, Inc, Mr.Nagasugi was the CEO for three organizations, Precision Direct, Authors Den and Digital Learning Management Corporation. Mr.Nagasugi received his BA in Economics from USC. He is a dynamic change agent with 20+ years portfolio of success in driving operations, strategic planning, business development, sales and marketing efforts for diverse organizations.
